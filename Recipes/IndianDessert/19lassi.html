Lassi

Jump to Recipe

Total Time

5 Minutes

Preparation Time

5 Minutes

Cook Time

5 Minutes

Chilling Time

10 Minutes


Ingredients

For Lassi

  • for punjabi lassi: 1 cup curd (thick) 2 tbsp sugar ¼ tsp cardamom powder
  • for dry fruit lassi: 1 cup curd (thick) 2 tbsp pistachios 2 tbsp almonds
  • 1 anjeer (chopped) 2 tbsp sugar ¼ tsp saffron food colour
  • for rose lassi: 1 cup curd (thick) 2 tbsp sugar 2 tbsp rooh afza
  • for chocolate lassi: 1 cup curd (thick) 3 tbsp chocolate sauce 2 tbsp sugar

Nutrition

  • Calories: 401kcal Carbohydrates: 41g Protein: 25g Fat: 16g Saturated Fat: 2g Cholesterol: 1mg
  • Sodium: 60mg Potassium: 116mg Fiber: 3g Sugar: 30g Vitamin A: 16IU Vitamin C: 1mg Calcium: 335mg Iron: 4mg


Instruction

Lassi

  • How to make punjabi lassi: firstly, in a blender take 1 cup curd. make sure the curd is thick and chilled. add 2 tbsp sugar and ¼ tsp cardamom powder. blend to make sure everything is well combined. finally, pour punjabi lassi in a tall glass and top with malai or cardamom powder.
  • How to make dry fruit lassi: firstly, in a blender take 1 cup curd. make sure the curd is thick and chilled. add 2 tbsp pistachios, 2 tbsp almonds, 1 anjeer, 2 tbsp sugar and ¼ tsp saffron food colour. blend to make sure everything is well combined. finally, pour dry fruit lassi in a tall glass and top with chopped pistachios.
  • How to make rose lassi: firstly, in a blender take 1 cup curd. make sure the curd is thick and chilled. add 2 tbsp sugar and 2 tbsp rooh afza. adjust the sugar to your choice as rooh afza also has sugar. blend to make sure everything is well combined. finally, pour rose lassi in a tall glass and top with rose syrup.
  • How to make chocolate lassi: firstly, in a blender take 1 cup curd. make sure the curd is thick and chilled. add 3 tbsp chocolate sauce and 2 tbsp sugar. blend to make sure everything is well combined. finally, pour chocolate lassi in a tall glass and top with chocolate syrup.
