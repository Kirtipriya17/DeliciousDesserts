Moti Chur Ka Lladdu

Jump to Recipe

Total Time

40 Minutes

Preparation Time

10 Minutes

Cook Time

30 Minutes

Chilling Time

10 Minutes


Ingredients

For Moti Chur Ka Laddu

  • 2 cup besan / gram flour
  • 2 tbsp rava / semolina / suji (fine)
  • ¼ tsp saffron food colour
  • 1½ cup water
  • oil for frying
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp cashew / kaju

Notes

  • firstly, add a generous amount of food colour to get a rich colour to ladoo. also, add milk to boondi mixture if the mixture is dry.
  • additionally, blend to boondi mixture to coarse powder if the boodi are not fine. finally, mootichoor ladoo recipe tastes good when prepared moist.


Instruction

Moti Chur Ka Laddu

  • firstly, to the hot sugar syrup, add prepared boondi.
  • mix well and make sure to coat all the boondi with sugar syrup.
  • continue to cook for 2 -3 minutes or until sugar syrup thickens.
  • cover and keep aside for 10 minutes.
  • after 10 minutes, sugar syrup is absorbed completely by boondi.
  • add 2 tbsp cashew and 2 tbsp pistachios. mix well.
  • you can see the the mixture is dried and all the sugar syrup has absorbed.
  • prepare the ladoo taking a small amount of boondi. add milk if required, to make moist laddu.
  • finally, motichoor laddo is ready to be served or refrigerate for a week.
