Besan Ka Halwa

Jump to Recipe

Total Time

40 Minutes

Preparation Time

5 Minutes

Cook Time

30 Minutes

Chilling Time

10 Minutes


Ingredients

For Besan Ka Halwa

  • ½ cup ghee / clarified butter
  • 1½ cup besan / gram flour
  • 2 tbsp rava / semolina / suji (fine)
  • 2 cup milk
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ¼ tsp cardamom powder
  • 2 tbsp saffron milk
  • 2 tbsp cashew / kaju (chopped)

Notes

  • firstly, roast the besan on low fame, else the halwa will taste raw. also, you can use water in place of milk if you are looking to store for a longer time.
  • additionally, add dry fruits of your choice to make more attractive. finally, besan halwa recipe or besan ka halwa recipe tastes great when prepared with ghee and milk.


Instruction

Besan Ka Halwa

  • firstly, in a large kadai take ½ cup ghee.once the ghee heats up, add in 1½ cup besan.
  • once the ghee heats up, add in 1½ cup besan.
  • also, add 2 tbsp rava. if you are using coarse besan then you can skip the addition of rava.
  • roast on low flame stirring continuously.
  • roast for almost 25-30 minutes or until the besan turns golden brown.
  • now add 2 cup milk and stir well.
  • keep stirring continuously, until the besan absorbs all the milk without forming lumps.
  • cook until the mixture separates the pan.
    • further, add ½ cup sugar and 2 tbsp saffron milk.
  • mix well until the sugar dissolves completely and separates the pan. further, add in 2 tbsp cashew and ¼ tsp cardamom powder. mix well. finally, enjoy besan ka halwa recipe garnished with more cashew.
