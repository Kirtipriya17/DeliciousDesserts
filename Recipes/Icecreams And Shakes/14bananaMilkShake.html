Banana Shake

Jump to Recipe

Total Time

10inutes

Preparation Time

10min

Chilling Time

2 Hours


Ingredients

For Banana Shake

  • 1 Cup Banana, chopped
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1/4 cup Cream (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla essence
  • 1/2 cup Ice (crushed)

Benefits of Banana Milkshake (Banana Shake)

  • Bananas being the most widely consumed fruit in the world has many benefits. Apart from being rich in fibre, bananas may help prevent asthma, cancer, high blood pressure, diabetes, cardiovascular disease, and digestive problems. The presence of potassium also reduces the risk of kidney stones. Combined with milk it work wonders since milk itself is high on a lot of nutrients like calcium which helps in maintaining bone density.
  • So basically this recipe of banana milkshake is an excellent drink for the kids to put on some nutrients in a delicious way which the would demand once they taste. A great way to drink up your fruits!


Instructions

Banana Milk Shake

  • Put all ingredients in a blender jar and blend smooth.
  • To make it thicker you can also add some milk powder
  • Pour the crushed ice on top.
  • Serve chilled.
