Chocolate Milk Shake

Jump to Recipe

Total Time

20 Minutes

Preparation Time

1O min

Baking Time

10 Minutes

Chilling Time

2 Hours


Instruction

For Cake

  • Combine the ice cream, diced chocolate, and 1/2 cup whipped cream in a blender. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses, and garnish with 1/4 cup whipped cream and chocolate shavings.

Nutrition Facts

  • 623 calories; protein 8.3g; carbohydrates 73.6g; fat 36.2g; cholesterol 73.2mg; sodium 158.3mg. Full Nutrition

about chocolate shake

  • Whipping cream makes this chocolate milkshake creamy and as light as air. Drizzle with chocolate syrup after you pour in the cream for an even more sensational chocolaty treat.


Ingredients

Chocolate Milk Shake

  • 2 cups premium quality chocolate ice cream
  • 2 (1.65 ounce) bars dark chocolate candy bars, diced
  • ½ cup whipped cream
  • 1 tablespoon chocolate shavings, for garnish
  • ¼ cup whipped cream
