Chocolate GolGappa

Jump to Recipe

Total Time

35 Minutes

Preparation Time

20 Minutes

Cook Time

5 minutes


Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 7 paani puris
  • 1 cup melted milk chocolate
  • 1/2 cup tutti-frutti
  • colorful sprinkles as required
  • 1 cup melted belgian chocolate
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips


Instructions

Preparation

  • Dip the pani puri in belgian chocolate In a bowl, add melted Belgian chocolate. Then take a plain pani puri, make a small hole in the center using your finger. Dip the pani puri in the melted chocolate. Make sure the chocolate gets coated well on the puri.
  • Freeze the pani puri Transfer the pani puris on parchment paper and add colorful sprinkles on the top and then let it freeze for at least 15 mins.
  • Start filling the pani puri Once your chocolate puris are made, start filling them by adding chocolate chips and tutti-frutti in the puris. Don't fill the puris with the stuffing. Just cover 1/3 part of it.
  • Pour the melted milk chocolate into the puri After adding chocolate chips and tutti-fruit, add melted milk chocolate. Don't fill the whole puri rather leave some space.
  • Your Chocolate Pani Puris are ready Finally, your chocolate pani puris are ready. You can add more toppings if you want. Enjoy these sweet delicacies.
  • Enjoy!
